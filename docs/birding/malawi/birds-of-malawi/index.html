Birding in Malawi

by Eugene Booysen

Malawi is one of Africa’s most rewarding yet underrated birding destinations. With over 650 species, including rare and endemic birds, the country offers an unparalleled experience for birdwatchers. Whether you’re exploring the lush miombo woodlands, vast wetlands, or scenic highlands, this guide will help you discover Malawi’s top birding locations, the best times to visit, and essential travel tips for an unforgettable birdwatching adventure.

"Do not waste time trying to find the meaning of life. Go find birds instead"
