Birding Tours in Zambia

​Zambia, located in the heart of southern Africa, boasts an impressive avian diversity with over 780 recorded bird species, making it a premier destination for birding enthusiasts. The country's varied ecosystems—including miombo woodlands, wetlands, and riverine forests—provide habitats for a multitude of bird species, some of which are endemic or near-endemic. Notably, Zambia is home to two endemic birds: the Chaplin's Barbet and the Black-cheeked Lovebird.
