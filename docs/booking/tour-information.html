Tour Information

South Africa is a dream destination for birders, home to an incredible diversity of habitats and over 850 recorded bird species, including many sought-after endemics. From the fynbos covered mountains of the Cape to the vast savannas of the Lowveld, our birding tours are designed to showcase the very best of the country's birding treasures. Whether you're chasing lifers or simply enjoying the thrill of discovery, we offer expertly guided tours that are immersive, flexible, and unforgettable. Join us as we explore South Africa's wetlands, grasslands, forests, and arid landscapes, uncovering a colorful world of birds against the backdrop of breathtaking scenery and vibrant wildlife.
