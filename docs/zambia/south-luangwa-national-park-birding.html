Birding in South Luangwa National Park

by Eugene Booysen

When people think of South Luangwa National Park in Zambia, iconic African wildlife like elephants, leopards, and lions typically come to mind. While it's undoubtedly a haven for big game and safari lovers, what often goes underappreciated is its status as one of Africa's premier birding destinations. With over 400 bird species recorded within its boundaries, South Luangwa is a birder's paradise nestled in the heart of Zambia's eastern province.
